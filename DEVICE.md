# CaPU as a Causal Device

CaPU is not a processor of commands.
It is a device that processes causes.

## Device Boundary
Input: Causal Records (vCML)
Output: Permitted Effects

## What CaPU Replaces
- Imperative execution
- Fire-and-forget actions
- Side effects without memory

## What CaPU Introduces
- Cause-before-effect
- Commit-before-execute
- Time-aware permission
